Sticky Notes for Gmail

Your notes are stored in your own Google Drive, within a private application folder reserved solely for this app. We never receive a copy, as we operate no servers for them to reach. They remain within your Google account, on Google’s infrastructure, and under your control.

Our handling of information obtained through Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

The app requires no email address to use. Should you choose to share one, you decide independently whether to receive news of the Sticky Notes app, of new apps from traumababies, both, or neither. You may unsubscribe from either at any time, and we do not sell your email address to unrelated third parties.

traumababies may sell or transfer the Sticky Notes app. In that event, subscribers to Sticky Notes updates may have their subscription passed to the new owner, who will identify itself and provide a means to unsubscribe; subscribers to traumababies updates remain with traumababies.

Should this policy change, the revised version will be published here, bearing a new date.
